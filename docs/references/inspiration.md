## 1. Experiential Benchmarks

We aim to capture the **feel** of the best penalty-shootout moments and lightweight sports games:

- The **tension** of a real penalty standoff: shooter vs. keeper, one decisive moment.
- The **satisfaction** of a clean strike and the **drama** of a save.
- The **pick-up-and-play** accessibility of casual mobile/desktop sports games.
- The **progression hook** of arcade sports titles (trophies, unlocks, "one more match").

## 2. Qualities Worth Emulating

| Quality | Why We Want It |
|---|---|
| **Instant readability** | Players understand the situation at a glance. |
| **Tactile feedback** | Every kick and save feels physical and rewarding. |
| **Short, replayable loops** | Encourages repeated sessions. |
| **Celebratory moments** | Wins feel earned and joyful. |
| **Clean, modern UI** | Signals quality and respects the player's attention. |

## 3. Anti-Patterns to Avoid

- Cluttered HUDs that bury the action.
- Slow or confusing menus that delay gameplay.
- Progression that feels grindy or arbitrary.
- Over-animated screens that distract rather than delight.
- Inconsistent controls or unpredictable navigation.

## 4. Tone & Personality

- Energetic, optimistic, and sporty — the buzz of match day.
- Confident and polished, never amateurish.
- Friendly to newcomers, rewarding for skilled players.

## 5. Notes for the Team

- Use these benchmarks as a "north star" during playtests: does a session *feel* tense, satisfying, and replayable?
- When in doubt, prioritize clarity and feel over feature breadth (quality over quantity).
